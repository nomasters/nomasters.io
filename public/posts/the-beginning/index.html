the beginning

January 20, 2018

The purpose of this project is to capture my thoughts and work on a journey towards living in accordance to a set of first principles revolving around freethought, skepticism, and libertarian socialism.

Because of my work as a technologist, this project will tend to cover topics that are technical in nature, but also explore guiding principles as they relate to the focus of this project.
